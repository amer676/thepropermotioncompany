# Software Development Contracts Guide

Every software project starts with a question. The quality of that question determines the quality of the outcome.

## The Core Problem

We have seen dozens of projects fail before a single line of code was written. The patterns are consistent and preventable.

At The Proper Motion Company, we start every project with a focused discovery phase. One call. One brief. Complete clarity.

## Our Approach

Here is what works:

- **Start with the outcome** — Define what success looks like before discussing technology

- **Design before building** — Architecture reviews save weeks of development time

- **Ship incrementally** — Weekly progress, not a grand reveal at the end

- **Measure and iterate** — Track business metrics, not just feature completion


We have used this framework across dozens of projects. It works because it is grounded in reality.

## What to Consider

The most expensive mistake in software development is building the wrong thing well. Validate before you invest.

## Key Takeaways

1. Requirements clarity prevents 80% of project failures.

2. Look for teams that ask hard questions, not just ones that say yes.

3. Plan for what happens after launch.
