# AI Document Processing for Business

AI has moved from experiment to infrastructure. The question is no longer whether to use it, but how to use it effectively.

## The Right Way to Integrate AI

At The Proper Motion Company, we follow a consistent approach:


1. **Start with the workflow** — Identify where humans spend time on pattern recognition, classification, or generation

2. **Design the integration** — Build AI into the existing workflow, not around it

3. **Plan for failure** — Every AI feature needs a graceful fallback path

4. **Measure the outcome** — Track whether the AI improved the business metric that matters


## Implementation Details

The best model is the one that reliably solves your specific problem at acceptable cost.

Key considerations:


- **Data quality** — Your AI is only as good as the data flowing into it

- **Latency** — Users expect fast responses. Design your AI pipeline accordingly

- **Cost management** — Token costs scale with usage. Caching and smart routing keep costs manageable

- **Monitoring** — AI features drift. Set up quality monitoring from day one


## Results

AI is not magic. It is a tool. When applied to the right problem with the right architecture, it delivers real results.
