// How we work

A process built
for real outcomes. ✦

No ambiguity, no surprises. Four phases, clear milestones, and a product you own completely at the end.

1
Week 1

Discovery & definition

We start by understanding exactly what you need. Not what sounds good, not what's easy to build — what you actually need.

2
Week 2–3

Architecture & design

We design the system before we build it. Wireframes, component structure, technical architecture. You review and approve. Changes happen here, not after.

3
Week 4–8

Development & iteration

We build in public-facing sprints. You see real progress every week, not a reveal at the end. Feedback is incorporated continuously.

4
Week 8+

Delivery & handoff

We ship. Then we hand over everything — code, documentation, credentials. You own it completely.
