// Philosophy

The name.

In astronomy, proper motion is the actual movement of a star across the sky — measured against the distant background, stripped of all apparent motion, all distortion, all noise. It's the true movement.

That is our philosophy. We build the actual thing. Not the appearance of it. Not a prototype dressed up as a product. The real thing, built properly.
